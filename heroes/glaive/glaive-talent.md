# Glaive Talent

## Rare Talent

![](../../.gitbook/assets/glaive_rare.png)

Using Afterburn \(A\) to punt a target will deal a portion of Afterburn's Damage and stun all enemies along its path.

| Duration | 0.5s |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Damage | 70% |
| Cooldown | 95% |
